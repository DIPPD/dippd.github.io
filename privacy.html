Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Dippd is a free Chrome extension that tracks product prices and alerts users when prices drop. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What Data We Collect

Dippd collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its service:

Email address (optional): If you choose to provide your email address, we use it solely to send you price drop alert notifications. Your email is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.

Product tracking data: When you track a product, we store the product title, URL, price, and your target price locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser unless you choose to provide your email for alerts.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

— Your browsing history
— Personal identification information
— Payment or financial information
— Location data
— Any data beyond what is listed above

How We Use Your Data

Your email address is used exclusively to send price drop notifications via EmailJS when a tracked product reaches your target price. We do not use your email for marketing, newsletters, or any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your tracked items and email address are stored locally on your device and are never shared with anyone.

Affiliate Links

When you click through to Amazon from Dippd, your link will include our Amazon Associates affiliate tag (dippd-20). This means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase. This comes at no extra cost to you and does not affect the prices you see.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the Dippd extension from Chrome.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
